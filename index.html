The Problem

Why Citizens Suffer in Silence

Millions of civic issues go unresolved every day. Here's why the current system fails citizens:

🌐

Language Barriers

Over 40% of citizens cannot file complaints because government portals only support English or Hindi, excluding hundreds of regional language speakers.

⏱️

Slow Response Times

Average complaint resolution takes 30–90 days due to manual processing, departmental handoffs, and lack of priority-based routing.

👁️

No Transparency

Citizens have no visibility into the status of their complaints. Complaints are filed and forgotten — with no updates, no accountability.

♿

Accessibility Issues

Elderly and differently-abled citizens cannot navigate complex online forms. Physical offices are inaccessible, especially in rural and semi-urban areas.

😤

Ignored Issues

Low-priority civic issues like broken streetlights, potholes, or open drains go unanswered for months, eroding public trust in government.

📋

Bureaucratic Chaos

Complaints are routed to the wrong departments, lost in inter-departmental transfers, or duplicated — resulting in citizen frustration and wasted resources.

Features That Matter Most

01

Voice Complaints in Any Language

Many Indian citizens are literate in their spoken language but not in written form. NagrikConnect's voice input system allows anyone to record a complaint verbally. Our speech recognition engine supports 12 regional languages and auto-transcribes the audio into a structured complaint form — no typing required.

  • ✅ Works on any smartphone
  • ✅ Background noise filtering
  • ✅ Auto-language detection
  • ✅ Instant transcription review
02

Photo & Document Evidence Upload

A picture is worth a thousand words — especially when reporting civic issues. Citizens can attach photographic evidence directly to their complaints. Our system automatically extracts GPS metadata, timestamps images, and stores them securely as immutable evidence — strengthening accountability for authorities.

  • ✅ Up to 5 images per complaint
  • ✅ GPS metadata extraction
  • ✅ Tamper-proof evidence storage
  • ✅ Drag-and-drop upload

Automatic Smart Escalation

If a complaint isn't resolved within its deadline, NagrikConnect automatically escalates it to the next level of authority — from local ward office to municipal commissioner to district collector. Citizens are notified at every escalation step, ensuring no issue is ever buried or ignored.

  • ✅ Multi-tier authority chain
